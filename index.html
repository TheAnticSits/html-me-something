Dudeism: Is The Dude the new Buddha

Dudeism is a religion loosely based on Taoism and Buddhism.

It was begun by a writer named Oliver Benjamin who noticed, while watching the film The Big Lebowski, that the main character, The Dude, exhibited all the characteristics of a Taoist. He is laid back, unassuming and lets things roll off of him.

The World's Slowest Growing Religion

Although it boasts to be the world's slowest growing religion there are an estimated 450,000 ordained Dudeist Priest's worldwide. In many locations being an ordained Dudeist gives you the ability to perform wedding ceremonies.
